Use Your Site to Make Money

If you’re interested in monetizing your blog through WordPress, there are simple, safe solutions for adding affiliate links to your page.

ThirstyAffiliates specializes in streamlining the affiliate links process by creating visually appealing links that connect your content to relevant products with a single click process. By taking the guess work out of utilizing affiliate links, ThirstyAffiliates also helps you manage your progress by providing accurate sales tracking (uses the Stats Add-on) and redirecting ugly affiliate links using your website URL.

If you’re looking to drive more sales to your store or site, Affiliates Manager offers another solution for e-Commerce plugins. Affiliates Manager helps you track your referrals and affiliates sent to your site so that you can give the appropriate commissions, and reports real-time traffic and sales so that you can track earnings and payouts. Best of all, it has no limits on affiliates – from 1 to 1,000, you can track them all.

Easy Affiliate Links is another solution for managing all of the affiliate links on your site. Using Easy Affiliate Links, you can create cloaks to your affiliate links so that they blend in with your content and create an easily accessible links using the visual and html editor. Future add-ons will include link analytics as well as geospecific links for regional and local sales support.

Community Engagement

Creating an interactive community on your WordPress blog is easy with forum plugins that allow visitors to communicate with each other and share ideas.

bbPress is a forum plug-in with a twist from the creators of WordPress. bbPress lets you easily setup discussion forums on your site. bbPress is easy to setup, allows for easy moderation, and provides a clean visual community element to your blog. bbPress full integrates with your central account and installs with one easy click. You can create multisite forums that allow you to divide your site into sections providing your users a chance to create their own content on your site.

For forums that focus on questions and answer, use the DW Question & Answer plugin to complete Q&A systems into your site that utilize resources like Quora and Stackoverflow to provide outside resources for your readers. DW Question & Answer allows your reader to submit questions that you can filter, order, edit, and even delete. Email notifications, instant search by keyword, and short codes make it easy to track and manage all the questions on your forum.

Allow your users to create custom profiles and build friendship connections using BuddyPress. User can engage in private conversations one on one or with several members at once. Even manage activity streams with members and groups with a site wide directory and get notified with user post on your site. BuddyPress allows your users to create micro-communities on your blog to maximize engagement and activity – right from your site.

Use directory plugins to turn your blog into a resource for listing and sharing other business profiles.

Business Directory Resources

For a Yelp-style review directory to link your users to on-brand and local businesses, Business Directory Plugin melds with the theme of your site to help you start a provider listing service. Using the Business Directory plugin you can build any kind of yellow-pages style directory to help your user find local resources that match the topic of your blog. Fully customizable form fields, plus the ability to upload photos and accept payment for listings helps turn your page into a robust directory for any kind of business listing.

For infinite scalability, GeoDirectory in the only WordPress plugin that allows you to add millions of listings to your blog, and utilizes premium add-ons to make monetizing and expanding your directory as easy as possible. Fully customizable listings help you create a compelling user experience that matches the quality and creativity of your site without being overcomplicated or sacrificing design or aesthetic.

Build a community driven, local business directory by using to Sabai Directory and give your users a chance to leave one to five-start reviews and bookmark listings for future reference. Fully interactive, Sabai Directory utilizes customizable maps to help your visitors find the closest resources to them and vote on photos and reviews to help others find high-quality local businesses using your site.

Calendar Support

Add booking systems into your WordPress sites to allow visitors to book appointments and see open availability, right from your page.

For easy online appointments, use BirchPress Scheduler to embed booking right into your page or posts via shortcode. Users on your site can check availability and then export appointments directly to their existing calendars including Google, iCal, and iPhone. Email notifications and reminders help your visitors set convent reminders for their upcoming sessions. Great for salons, physical therapy, and financial services.

Communicate directly with service providers using GravityForums. Secure, accessible, and flexible, GravityForums has been optimized for mobile devices to provide convenient communication between your users and local business providers. Extend GravityForums to integrate with other popular web apps and online services to create the complete booking and buying solution for your visitors.

For customizable, responsive booking layouts, Bookly provides one of the most robust appointment booking plugins for your WordPress site. With built-in shopping carts and WooCommerce integration, Bookly provides a full customizable appearance to set up reservations quickly and easily right from your page.

Property Bloggin’

For a unique WordPress application, use specialized plugins to integrate real estate support directly into your WordPress blog.

For creating and managing highly customizable real estate, property management, and custom showcase listings, WP-Property is the leading WordPress plugin. With more downloads than any other real estate plugin, WP-Property has multiple diverse applications, letting you list entities from properties to products with ease and flexibility. With no coding required, you can specify search criteria and sort results with a single click. With deep customization levels, the versatility of WP-Property limited only by your imagination.

If you want to use your WordPress blog to create a dynamic real estate website in minutes with advanced features, shortcakes and templates, let Easy Property Listings help you build your page faster than ever. Using Easy Property Listings, you can create beautiful listings in a fully responsive list or grid format. With hundreds of ready to use theme templates available for free, you can craft your page to match your company branding with ease. With mobile functionality, your visitors can access your content from anywhere.

For further MLS integration, use WPL Real Estate for flexibility in design for real estate and vertical markets. WPL Real Estate allows you add, remove, and change fields without having to customize the database. Data management tools and premium add-on options include support for cloud hosting and listhub integration.

Video Elements

Expand the versatility of your page by adding YouTube and other self-hosted videos into your content.

Easily manage and merge multiple videos on your blog using TubePress. You can link YouTube channels, playlists, and individual videos creatively using TubePress. By utilizing short codes, you can embed videos anywhere on your site, letting you customize their placement and visualization to match your existing theming. Link to other video hosting site like Vimeo or Dailymotion to expand your video content offerings.

Build your own WordPress gallery using Envira Gallery, an easy and powerful WordPress plugin. Create beautiful video galleries in just minutes using Envira. Maximized for mobile, tablet, and desktop platforms, Envira Gallery makes videos on your blog look great for all of your visitors, regardless of their viewing preference. The drag and drop gallery builder makes creating unique content simple and easy.

Give your visitors a chance to leave their own comments directly on your WordPress site using YouTube Showcase. Designed and developed to be used without writing a single line of code, the YouTube Showcase plugin is simple and easy to use. Build galleries as well as content and excerpts to allow your user to leave unique comments on your blog and create new ways for them to interact with each other.

Classifieds

Increase commerce and monetization opportunities by adding classified ads to your blog or site.

Add classified ads to your page using shortcakes that work with most themes out of the box using Classifieds WP. Upload multiple images for each classified using that native browser, and organize them with categories and types. Streamline the process by allowing advertisers to preview their listings before the post goes live, and contact them by phone or email based off their preferences once the listing is submitted. Change the themes with built in shortcakes to match the theme and subject of your blog.

WPAdverts lets you instal classifieds with a single click. Whether you're building your blog for the first time, or adding classifieds to an existing page, WPAdverts uses simplified short codes to see up your ads like a professional. Formatted to be constantly compatible with the newest versions of WordPress, WPAdverts works with every properly coded WordPress theme, letting your customize the looks of your classifieds to your blog for seamless integration. Utilize third-party add-ons like Yoast SEO, Jetpack, and others to create increased functionality based on your the user experience you’ve created for your visitors.

With world class support and a worldwide community of users, you’re never far from the answers if you use Another WordPress Classifieds Plugin. With great out of the box features, and over 70,000 unique downloads, AWPCP is simple to configure, but powerful to use. Premium add-ons allow you to maximize your classifieds and include additional payment options for your users.

Rally Your Raising

Rally your users behind a product of cause by adding crowding funding links to your existing blog content.

For a complete, all-in-one solution, IgnitionDeck offers their unique crowdfunding framework and works with any of the thousands of themes available with WordPress. IgnitionDeck is the only professional grade crowdfunding platform for WordPress and enables flexible donation amounts for complete customization. Utilize real-time stats and brilliantly designed front and backend displays to track your goals and contributors. Integration with third-party partners like PayPal and Mailchimp, as well as social media platforms create an exceptional experience for you and your users.

Collect donations, crowdfund, or kickstart a project with WordPress Fundraising. Allow your users to pledge a single contribution or elect for recurring donations to continue supporting your product or cause. Customize goals, amount, and fundraiser lengths with built-in options and styles. Funding progress bar helps you and your visitors track progress to the goal and supports 100 different plugins and themes.

Charitable is the Wordpress fundraising toolkit that gives you complete control over your online fundraising. Help your users support a common cause by installing an unlimited number of fundraising campaigns, each in less than five minutes. Utilize PayPal and offline payments or considering a premium payment add-on like Stripe or PayFast to tailor your crowdfunding needs to your existing or new content.

WikiBlog

Help your users find quick answers and resources to their questions using your blog.

WP Knowledgebase lets your create an attractive and professional knowledge base, forum, and FAQ hub for your visitors to navigate their way to solutions using predictive text search engines to simplify the searching process. Fully customizable, you can chose to display comments or sort the knowledge base articles to create a community of engagement around your existing content. Works across all major browsers, creating a user friendly design for both you and your users.

Based on the WordPress and AJAX search box, DeskPress is a smart search plugin for FAQ’s, knowledge bases, and bbPress forums. Create new topics simply and with ease, or utilize the bbPress Forum as a support help desk system if you get stuck. DeskPress lets you send private replies to your visitors, and allow access features for sensitive information. Email notifications and optional moderators on each forum or topic help you maintain control of the knowledgebase quickly and securely. Live suggestions and Best Answer features help your users sort through submissions to find the best possible information.
